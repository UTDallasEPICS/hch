Forms
    form_id (PK)
    form_name

Questions
    question_id (PK)
    form_id (FK) — Links to Forms
    question_text
    question_type (Enum: Text, Multiple Choice, etc.)

QuestionOptions (New table for 3NF)
Why: For Multiple Choice/Dropdowns, you need a place to store the "choices." Storing them in an array in Questions violates 1NF.
    option_id (PK)
    question_id (FK)
    option_text

Responses
    submission_id (PK) — Groups all answers from one form submission
    patient_id (FK)
    form_id (FK)
    submitted_at (Timestamp)

Answers
    answer_id (PK)
    submission_id (FK) — Links to the specific response event
    question_id (FK)
    answer_text — Used for 'Text' and 'Date' types
    option_id (FK) — Optional: Links to QuestionOptions for 'Multiple Choice/Checkbox'

Users
    user_id (PK)
    email
    name
    phone_number
    role

Staff
    staff_id (PK/FK) — Points back to User_id
    title

Patient
    patient_id (PK/FK) — Points back to User_id
    start_date
    end_date
    status (waitlist, active, inactive)

Note: I removed current_week_count because it is derived from start_date and violates 3NF.

Session
    session_id (PK) — Every table needs its own unique key
    patient_id (FK)
    staff_id (FK)
    notes_id (FK)
    attendance (Enum)
    date_time
    location (Enum: online/in-person)
    is_recurring (Bool)
    frequency

Notes
    notes_id (PK)
    content
    is_locked
    signed_at
